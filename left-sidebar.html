My Short Bio

I should start by saying that I struggled with addiction/alcoholism most of my life up until almost three years ago and that kept me from ever finding a true career. But that time brought its own special brand of experience and knowledge in life, but I finally had had enough so two and half years ago I decided to get clean and see what a sober and motivated version of myself could make of this life. I love challenges and being part of things that are groundbreaking and innovative. I am currently enjoying learning the skills to become a full-stack software engineer, but my new passion lies with blockchain and crypto development because I believe it will be a major game-changer in the industry and we have the chance to get in at its infancy!
