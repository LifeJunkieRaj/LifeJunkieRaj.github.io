My Full-Stack Skills

I'm fully proficient in Javascript, HTML and CSS. I've coded two fully functioning games solo, one being a simple Battleship game that incorporated both Javascript and Readline. The other was a very simple version of the "Guess the Number" game which was coded completely in Python.

I helped code in two group projects one of which was in Express and Sequelize and the other was in React and Flask. Both of these helped me realize that the advantages of working in a group. You can absolutely get more done in a more efficient way if everyone is communicating, listening and most of all, sharing that sense of collaboration.

My final project was done in React and Flask as well. By the time I finished this project I knew that I'd found the career I want to pursue and grow in for the rest of my life. I had created a unique website that I think meets a very specific need that many these days are looking to get met, but have no idea of how to even get started. And the fact that I created that from scratch is a powerful and fulfilling feeling to me even now. I can't wait to further my experience and skills in software engineering while honing my current skills as well.
